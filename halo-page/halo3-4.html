HALO 3

The opening scene to Halo 3 was most memorable to me, and a campaign I played to the very end. Master Chief and the arbiter work together to fight off the brutes. For a good portion of Halo 3, you do not have Cortana with you. Cortana was left behind in Halo 2. Master Chief is seeing glimpses of her. Master Chief works with Guitly Spark 343, to fix a recording that Cortana left behind for chief. The message left behind tells chief that there is something important beyond the portal. Some believe that Cortana has been corrupted by the flood, but the elites, chief and Sargent Johnson head into the portal anyway. To my bewilderment, you work with these mutated flood to kill the prophet Truth.
After he is dead, and it is revealed that Guilty Spark 343 is building a new halo ring, the mission is to go to the control room and destroy the new halo ring by activating it. Sargent Johnson is killed by Guilty Spark 343. Chief kills Guilty Spark and activates the Halo, escaping with the Arbiter on Johnson’s warthog. The only one that makes it off the ring is the Arbiter. Chief goes into a cryopod in the back of the broken Forward unto Dawn, and tells Cortana to “wake me, when you need me.”


My Review

Halo 3. Phenominial. Amazing. What more can I say? The play through felt long, but it was worth it. There are so many great funny moments and memorable memes from this game. 10/10
