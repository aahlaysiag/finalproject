HALO 4

Halo 4 opens with explanation of the Spartans from Halsey who is being interrogated about why they are originally built. It then moves on to 4 years later after the Halo being destroyed. UNSC Forward unto Dawn holds Master Chief and Cortana. As requested, she wakes Chief. The graphics are stunning, and honestly was the most pleasing to eye for me. The story line confused me a bit though. I would like to add Halo 4 was developed by 343 Industries, instead of Bungie (whom previously worked on the Halo games).
In summary, Cortana is going into AI rampancy. She’s been in service for 8 years, and the expiration date on an AI is 7. Chief believes that if they can find Halsey, Cortana can be fixed. But they’ve landed on a new planet. A forerunner planet called Requiem. They meet a new foe, a forerunner named Didact, whom has unleashed an army of promethians and destroy humanity. Cortana sacrifices herself to save Earth and send the Master Chief back home.


My Review

I felt like this game was short. Not that I have an issue with it being short, it was a great, emotional game. I felt like the combat was a bit more difficult, but other than that. It was great. Intersting relationship between Cortana and Chief. 9/10
