HALO 5: Guardians

Halo 5 opens with Fire Team Osiris - Spartan IV’s Locke, Tanaka, Buck, and Vale preparing to hunt down Blue Team - John, Frederic, Linda, and Kelly. Just like Halo 2, the player switches between Locke and Master Chief. The mission for Osiris is to hunt down the AWOL Blue team, and Blue Teams mission is to find Cortana. Cortana uses these Forerunner tech called Guardians to different parts of the universe. She uses them to police worlds. Cortana tells blue team, on a planet named Genesis, that she has been cured of her rampancy and has been saved by the Forerunner technology. Osiris catches up with Blue Team, and its revealed Cortana is going to use the Guardians to force the Galaxy into a peace through disarmament. John tries to persuade Cortana to abandon the plan and return with them, but she refuses.
Osiris is able to help Exuberant get control of Genesis back, and they rescue Blue Team from Cortana, but are unable to prevent the Guardians and Cortana from leaving. Many AIs throughout the galaxy join Cortana’s Created faction. Cortana tries to disable the Infinity, but Roland, the ship’s AI is still loyal to humanity, executes a random slipspace jump and is told to do that until they can fight back against Cortana. Locke takes John to Sanghelios where they reunite with Arbiter and Halsey.


My Review

Apparently, Halo 5 was hated due to the gameplay and story changing. I enjoyed it, and just took the gameplay as the developers trying to try something new, while still keeping it natural halo. My only critque would be the advertisement, it was a bit misleading. But either way, it was still fun and had a great plot. 10/10
